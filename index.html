About Me

Resourceful. Resilient. Creative when it counts.

I am a systems-oriented infrastructure and network operations professional focused on keeping critical environments stable, functional, and well coordinated. I approach issues with a systems-thinking mindset, working problems methodically through remote troubleshooting, dispatching field technicians, guiding on-site staff through repairs, or coordinating multi-team calls until resolution.
